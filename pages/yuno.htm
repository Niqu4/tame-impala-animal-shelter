Meet Yuno, the Shiba Inu
Greetings, my name is Yuno. Puppies like me are a lot of fun. I will provide you with hours of entertainment and a lifetime of love. I’m hoping you can teach me how to be an important part of your family. I’ll need lessons on housebreaking, chewing, and basic manners. It shouldn’t take me very long to learn everything because you will love me and be very patient.
